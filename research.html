Showcase Project 2: Classifying Small Cell Lung Cancer Cell Phenotypes Using Machine Learning

In Summer 2021, I worked under Dr. Mohamed Abazeed, MD PhD, in his Radiation Oncology lab at the Feinberg School of Medicine. My job as an undergraduate researcher was to write a machine learning algorithm that would be able to segment and classify cells in gray scale histology images using machine learning imaging techniques such as optical flow and k-means clustering.

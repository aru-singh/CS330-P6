Showcase Project 1: Project MED's Healthcare Opportunity Database

Project MED is a nonprofit that a few Northwestern students and I started two years ago as a way to help students that hold historically underrepresented idenities in medicine explore their interest in healthcare and guide them through working towards those careers. Below is a database we created to have a centralized place for these students to seek healthcare opportunities such as shadowing, research, clinical expererience and more in the Chicagoland area and beyond.
